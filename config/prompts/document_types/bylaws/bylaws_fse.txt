ARTICLE III: DIRECTORS
Section 1. Number. The corporation shall have no less than three (3) and no more than seven (7) directors.

Section 1. Officers. The officers of the corporation shall be a President, a Secretary, and a Chief Financial Officer, who shall act as treasurer.

ARTICLE VI: INDEMNIFICATION
