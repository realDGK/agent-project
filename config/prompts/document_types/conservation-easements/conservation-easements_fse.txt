DEED OF CONSERVATION EASEMENT

This Deed is made October 1, 2031, by Green Valley Ranch LLC ("Grantor") to the North Coast Land Trust ("Grantee").

Grantor conveys to Grantee a perpetual conservation easement over the 500-acre property described in Exhibit A to protect its scenic and wildlife habitat values.

Prohibited uses include any subdivision of the property, commercial timber harvesting, and construction of any new structures outside of the designated building envelope.
