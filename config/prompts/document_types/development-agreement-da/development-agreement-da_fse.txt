DEVELOPMENT AGREEMENT
(Northgate Specific Plan)

This Development Agreement is entered into on January 10, 2030, between the City of Northgate ("City") and Northgate Partners, LLC ("Developer").

1. Project: This DA governs the development of the 200-acre Northgate Specific Plan area.
2. Term: The term of this agreement is twenty (20) years.
3. Vested Rights: Developer's right to develop the Project in accordance with existing regulations is hereby vested.
4. Developer Obligations: Developer shall construct the public park and arterial roadway improvements as described in Exhibit C.
5. Public Benefits: The Project will provide needed housing and generate significant tax revenue.
