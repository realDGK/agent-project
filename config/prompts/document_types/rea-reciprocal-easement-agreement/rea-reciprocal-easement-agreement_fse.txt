DECLARATION OF RECIPROCAL EASEMENTS FOR PINE RIDGE SHOPPING CENTER

This Declaration is made December 1, 2028, by Shopping Center Developer LLC, owner of the property known as "The Shoppes at Pine Ridge".

This REA hereby establishes for the benefit of all parcels within the shopping center non-exclusive, reciprocal easements for vehicular and pedestrian access, parking, and utility lines across all common areas.

The Owner's Association shall be responsible for the maintenance and repair of all common areas. Costs for such maintenance shall be allocated among the parcel owners on a pro-rata basis based on the gross leasable area of their respective buildings.

Recorded December 2, 2028, as Instrument No. 2028-445566.
