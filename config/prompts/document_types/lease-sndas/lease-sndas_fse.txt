SUBORDINATION, NON-DISTURBANCE, AND ATTORNMENT AGREEMENT

This SNDA is made as of May 10, 2028, by and among BIG BANK, N.A. ("Lender"), MAIN STREET PROPERTIES LLC ("Landlord"), and THE COFFEE SPOT LLC ("Tenant").

RECITALS:
A. Landlord and Tenant entered into a Lease dated June 1, 2025.
B. Lender has made a loan to Landlord evidenced by a Deed of Trust recorded concurrently herewith.

AGREEMENT:
1. Subordination. The Lease is and shall be subordinate to the lien of the Deed of Trust.
2. Non-Disturbance. So long as Tenant is not in default, Lender agrees not to disturb Tenant's possession in the event of a foreclosure.
3. Attornment. Tenant agrees to attorn to and recognize Lender as the new landlord in the event of a foreclosure.
