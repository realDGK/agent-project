AGREEMENT CONTAINING COVENANTS AFFECTING REAL PROPERTY
(Inclusionary Housing Program)

This Agreement is made May 1, 2030, by and between Affordable Housing Partners LLC ("Owner") and the City of Bayview ("City").

The Owner agrees to set aside and restrict twenty (20) of the residential units at the "Bayview Towers" project as affordable to households earning at or below 80% of the Area Median Income (AMI).

These restrictions shall remain in effect for a period of fifty-five (55) years from the date of the Certificate of Occupancy.
