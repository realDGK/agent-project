RIGHT OF FIRST REFUSAL AGREEMENT

This Agreement is made November 5, 2027, between City Parking Corp. ("Grantor") and Downtown Properties LLC ("Holder").

1. Grant of Right. Grantor hereby grants to Holder a Right of First Refusal ("ROFR") on the real property located at 45 Central Ave.
2. Trigger. If Grantor receives a bona fide offer from a third party to purchase the property, Grantor must provide written notice of the offer to Holder.
3. Exercise. Holder shall have fifteen (15) business days after receipt of the notice to elect to purchase the property on the same terms and conditions as the third-party offer.
